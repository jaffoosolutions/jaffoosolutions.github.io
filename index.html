Who We Are

We specialise in cloud migration strategies to drive cloud-native transformation of business application, network and security.

With a breadth of experience delivering infrastructure and software on greenfield and brownfield cloud migration projects, across both public and private sectors, Yassine Jaffoo founded Jaffoo Solutions Ltd in 2019 to help businesses harness the resiliency, elasticity and scalability of Cloud.

We build on proven principles, patterns and processes to architect and engineer solutions that enable businesses to leverage the benefits of Cloud and support their go-to-market strategies.
